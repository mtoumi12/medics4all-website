The Global Healthcare Language Crisis

Communication Barriers

25.7M+ US patients with limited English proficiency can't effectively communicate with their doctors due to language barriers, leading to medical errors and poor outcomes.

Documentation Burden

Doctors spend 75% of their scheduled time on EHR and documentation tasks, with most occurring outside clinical hours, reducing actual patient care time.

Compliance Costs

Healthcare systems face legal liability for untranslated materials. Professional interpreter services cost $45-150 per hour, with litigation damages reaching millions for language access failures.

$8.4 Billion

Projected MENA healthcare AI market by 2033, with growing demand for multilingual medical documentation solutions across Arabic-speaking regions
